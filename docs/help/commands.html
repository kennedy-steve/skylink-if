/register-meâ€‹

Register your Infinite Flight account with Skylink-IF. This will allow your friends and servers to see your activity on Infinite Flight. You'll also be able to unlock future features!

Ground Crew Verificationâ€‹

When you register, Skylink-IF will Direct Message you instructions for verification. You will be asked to spawn in Infinite Flight with a certain livery and True Heading. Our ground crew will then verify that you followed the instructions we gave you. This process may take a couple of minutes, and Skylink-IF will notify you when it's complete. This process prevents abuse of registration.

/enable-notificationsâ€‹

Enable notifications for a channel. You can specify the type of notifications and channel. The current notification types are for Active Pilot and Active Controller. A notification is only sent when a user, who can see the channel, is online on Infinite Flight either as a Pilot or as an ATC Controller.
